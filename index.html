Makoporo Ntwala Safaris is a premier tour and travel company based in Uganda, dedicated to offering authentic, immersive, and unforgettable safari experiences across East Africa.

From the lush rainforests and serene lakes of Uganda to the vast savannas of Tanzania and Kenya, we pride ourselves on delivering well-rounded and expertly curated journeys that capture the essence of African adventure and cultural richness.

Our Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kampala

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport and transfer to your hotel in Kampala.

Day 2: Murchison Falls National Park

Travel to Murchison Falls and enjoy a game drive and boat cruise.

Day 3: Queen Elizabeth National Park

Explore the park and witness the tree-climbing lions.

Day 4: Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Embark on a gorilla trekking adventure.

Day 5: Departure

Transfer back to Entebbe for your departure flight.
